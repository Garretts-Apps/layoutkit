Tutorial

Five tags, start to finish. Every example below is rendered live by the stylesheet — view source, there's no JavaScript.
Step 1

Center a div

The CSS final boss. One tag, both axes.

<lk-center>
  <div class="chip">centered</div>
</lk-center>
centered
Step 2

Stack with consistent spacing

lk-stack is a vertical flex column; gap sets the rhythm.

<lk-stack gap="md">
  <div class="chip">one</div>
  <div class="chip">two</div>
</lk-stack>
one
two
three
Step 3

A header with lk-spread

Push children to opposite ends — logo left, links right.

<lk-spread align="center">
  <strong>Logo</strong>
  <lk-row gap="sm"><a>Home</a><a>About</a></lk-row>
</lk-spread>
LogoHomeAbout
Step 4

A responsive grid

responsive auto-fits columns by available width — no breakpoints. Resize the window.

<lk-grid responsive gap="md" style="--lk-min-child-width: 140px">
  <div class="chip">card</div> …
</lk-grid>
card 1
card 2
card 3

Step 5

Compose a page

fill grows a region to take the remaining space — header, content, done.

<lk-stack style="height: 200px">
  <lk-spread padding="sm"><strong>App</strong><a>Menu</a></lk-spread>
  <lk-box fill><lk-center>Content</lk-center></lk-box>
</lk-stack>
